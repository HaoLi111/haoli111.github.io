About Me

I am a researcher and developer with a strong focus on mathematical modeling, data science, and computational mathematics. My work spans across multiple domains including numerical computation, statistical analysis, and scientific visualization.

With expertise in R, Julia, and Python, I develop tools and libraries that make complex mathematical concepts accessible and practical for research and education.

Research Interests

Mathematical Modeling

Development of mathematical models for real-world applications, including aerospace dynamics and optimization problems.

Data Science & Statistics

Statistical analysis, machine learning, and comprehensive data exploration across various datasets.

Numerical Computation

Implementation of numerical methods and algorithms for scientific computing in Julia and R.

Scientific Visualization

Creating interactive visualizations for mathematical concepts, fractals, and dynamical systems.

Aerospace & Aviation

Model aircraft design, exploratory aerospace calculations, and power system analysis.

Computational Biology

Classification algorithms and dichotomous key systems for biological taxonomy.
